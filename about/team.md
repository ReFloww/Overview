## Culture & Values

### Transparency

We believe in radical transparency. Our smart contracts are open source, our processes are documented, and we communicate openly with our community.

### Security First

User funds and data security are our top priorities. We implement best practices in smart contract development and undergo regular security audits.

### User-Centered Design

We build for real users. Every feature is designed to solve actual problems and provide genuine value to lenders and partners.

### Continuous Improvement

We iterate rapidly based on user feedback and market needs. The product evolves with our community.
