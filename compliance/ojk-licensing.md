# OJK Licensing

Restock.id, ReFlow's P2P lending partner, operates under the supervision of OJK (Otoritas Jasa Keuangan) - Indonesia's Financial Services Authority.

## About OJK

### What is OJK?

OJK (Otoritas Jasa Keuangan) is Indonesia's integrated financial services regulator. Established in 2011, it oversees:

- Banking
- Capital markets
- Insurance
- Pension funds
- Other financial services
- **Financial technology (including P2P lending)**

### OJK's Role in P2P Lending

OJK regulates the P2P lending industry through:
- Licensing requirements
- Operating standards
- Consumer protection
- Supervision and monitoring
- Enforcement actions
