## Solution 1: Tokenized Loans

### How It Works

Every P2P loan is converted into tradeable ERC-20 tokens:

```
Loan Created → Tokens Minted → Distributed to Investors
                                      ↓
                            Tokens in Your Wallet
                                      ↓
                            Trade, Hold, or Sell
```

### The Transformation

| Before (Traditional) | After (ReFlow) |
|---------------------|----------------|
| Locked position | Tradeable token |
| Illiquid | Liquid |
| Paper record | On-chain asset |
| Platform-controlled | Self-custodied |

### Token Properties

- **Ownership**: Represents your share of the loan
- **Fungible**: Each token is interchangeable
- **Tradeable**: Can be sold on secondary market
- **Verifiable**: Ownership provable on blockchain
- **Automated**: Receives distributions automatically

## Solution 3: On-Chain Transparency

### How It Works

Every transaction is recorded on the Mantle blockchain:

```
Every Action → Blockchain Record → Publicly Verifiable
    ↓               ↓                    ↓
Investment    Transaction Hash    Check on Explorer
Distribution      Block #          Verify yourself
Trade            Timestamp         No trust needed
```

### What You Can Verify

| Activity | Verification |
|----------|-------------|
| Your investment | Token balance in wallet |
| Distributions | Transaction history |
| Platform operations | Contract interactions |
| Token supply | Total supply on contract |
| Any transaction | Look up tx hash |

### Eliminating Opacity

```
Traditional: Trust the platform
ReFlow: Trust the blockchain

Don't trust, verify.
```

## Solution 5: Non-Custodial Architecture

### How It Works

Your assets stay in your control:

```
Traditional:
Your Money → Platform Account → Platform Controls

ReFlow:
Your Money → Your Wallet → You Control
                ↓
        Smart Contracts (Not platform)
                ↓
        Automated, trustless operations
```

### Custody Comparison

| Risk | Traditional | ReFlow |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Platform failure | Funds at risk | Funds safe in wallet |
| Platform hack | May lose funds | Limited exposure |
| Regulatory action | Funds frozen | In your control |
| Exit | Platform approval | Your decision |
