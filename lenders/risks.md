# Risks and Considerations

All investments carry risk. Understanding the risks of P2P lending on ReFlow helps you make informed decisions and manage your portfolio appropriately.

## Investment Risks

### 1. Credit Risk (Default Risk)

**What It Is**: Borrowers may fail to repay their loans.

**Impact**:
- Partial or total loss of principal
- Lost interest income
- Recovery process takes time

**Mitigation**:
- Diversify across multiple loans
- Favor higher-grade loans (A, B)
- Review borrower sector health
- Don't invest more than you can afford to lose

### 2. Liquidity Risk

**What It Is**: You may not be able to sell tokens when needed.

**Impact**:
- Tokens stuck until maturity
- May need to sell at discount
- Timing flexibility reduced

**Mitigation**:
- Don't invest emergency funds
- Keep some capital liquid
- Understand secondary market depth
- Diversify maturities

### 3. Interest Rate Risk

**What It Is**: Market rates may change after you invest.

**Impact**:
- Locked into lower rate if rates rise
- Opportunity cost
- May affect secondary market value

**Mitigation**:
- Ladder investments across durations
- Stay informed about rate trends
- Consider shorter durations for flexibility

### 4. Concentration Risk

**What It Is**: Too much invested in one loan, sector, or grade.

**Impact**:
- Single default significantly hurts portfolio
- Sector downturn affects multiple investments
- Correlated losses

**Mitigation**:
- Limit per-loan allocation (5-10% max)
- Spread across sectors
- Mix risk grades appropriately
- Regular rebalancing

## Platform Risks

### 5. Smart Contract Risk

**What It Is**: Bugs or vulnerabilities in smart contracts.

**Impact**:
- Loss of funds
- Contract malfunction
- Unexpected behavior

**Mitigation**:
- ReFlow conducts security audits
- Start with smaller amounts
- Monitor platform announcements
- Stay informed about any issues

### 6. Technology Risk

**What It Is**: System failures, hacks, or technical problems.

**Impact**:
- Temporary loss of access
- Transaction delays
- Potential security breach

**Mitigation**:
- Use secure wallet practices
- Enable all security features
- Keep software updated
- Never share private keys

### 7. Operational Risk

**What It Is**: Issues with ReFlow or partner operations.

**Impact**:
- Service disruptions
- Processing delays
- Communication breakdowns

**Mitigation**:
- Choose verified platforms
- Monitor platform status
- Maintain documentation
- Have backup access methods

## Partner Risks

### 8. Restock.id Risk

**What It Is**: Risk associated with the P2P lending partner.

**Impact**:
- Loan origination issues
- Collection problems
- Regulatory compliance

**Mitigation**:
- Restock.id is OJK-licensed
- Established operating history
- Regulatory oversight
- Due diligence on partnership

## Market Risks

### 9. Secondary Market Risk

**What It Is**: Market conditions affecting token trading.

**Impact**:
- Wide bid-ask spreads
- Difficulty finding buyers
- Price volatility

**Mitigation**:
- Don't rely solely on secondary market
- Price realistically when selling
- Understand market liquidity
- Plan for holding to maturity

### 10. Regulatory Risk

**What It Is**: Changes in laws affecting platform operations.

**Impact**:
- Operating restrictions
- New compliance requirements
- Potential service changes

**Mitigation**:
- Platform monitors regulations
- Geographic restrictions in place
- Compliance-first approach
- Stay informed of changes

## Blockchain Risks

### 11. Network Risk

**What It Is**: Issues with Mantle Network or blockchain infrastructure.

**Impact**:
- Transaction delays
- Network congestion
- Bridge issues

**Mitigation**:
- Mantle is established L2
- Multiple RPC endpoints
- Patient during congestion
- Check network status

### 12. Wallet Security Risk

**What It Is**: Losing access to your wallet or credentials.

**Impact**:
- Total loss of funds
- Irreversible

**Mitigation**:
- Secure backup of seed phrase
- Hardware wallet for large amounts
- Never share private keys
- Verify URLs carefully

## Risk Management Best Practices

### Before Investing

1. **Assess Your Risk Tolerance**
   - How much can you afford to lose?
   - What's your investment horizon?
   - How would losses affect you?

2. **Educate Yourself**
   - Understand P2P lending
   - Learn about blockchain
   - Read all documentation

3. **Start Small**
   - Test with small amounts
   - Learn the platform
   - Scale up gradually

### While Investing

1. **Diversify**
   - Multiple loans
   - Multiple sectors
   - Multiple grades
   - Staggered maturities

2. **Monitor Regularly**
   - Check portfolio weekly
   - Review loan status
   - Watch for alerts

3. **Stay Informed**
   - Platform announcements
   - Market conditions
   - Regulatory news

### Portfolio Guidelines

| Risk Level | Grade Distribution | Max Per Loan |
|------------|-------------------|--------------|
| Conservative | 80% A/B, 20% C | 5% |
| Moderate | 50% A/B, 40% C, 10% D | 7% |
| Aggressive | 30% A/B, 40% C, 30% D/E | 10% |

## What to Do If Things Go Wrong

### Loan Default

1. Don't panic
2. Check recovery status
3. Document for taxes
4. Learn and adjust strategy

### Platform Issues

1. Document the issue
2. Contact support
3. Check official channels
4. Verify through blockchain

### Wallet Compromise

1. Transfer remaining funds immediately
2. Revoke all approvals
3. Create new wallet
4. Report incident
