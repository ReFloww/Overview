# The Problem We Solve

Traditional P2P lending has significant limitations that hurt both lenders and borrowers. ReFlow addresses these fundamental issues.

## The Current State of P2P Lending

### Market Overview

P2P lending has grown significantly, connecting borrowers directly with lenders. But despite its promise, the industry faces structural problems that limit its potential.

### The Core Issues

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│              TRADITIONAL P2P LENDING PROBLEMS                │
├─────────────┬─────────────┬─────────────┬──────────────────┤
│  ILLIQUIDITY │  OPACITY    │  ACCESS     │  CUSTODY RISK   │
│  Funds locked│  Black box  │  Limited    │  Platform holds │
```

## Problem 1: Illiquidity

### What Happens Today

When you invest in a traditional P2P loan:
- Funds locked for 6-12 months (or longer)
- No way to exit early
- Emergencies? Too bad
- Opportunity cost if better options appear

### Real Impact

| Scenario | Traditional P2P | Impact |
|----------|-----------------|--------|
| Need cash urgently | Can't access | Miss opportunities or face hardship |
| Found better investment | Stuck | Opportunity cost |
| Loan performing poorly | Trapped | Ride it out |
| Market conditions change | No flexibility | Can't adapt |

### The Numbers

```
Average loan term: 12 months
Funds locked: 100% until maturity
Early exit option: None
```

## Problem 2: Opacity

### What Happens Today

Most P2P platforms operate as black boxes:
- Limited visibility into loan performance
- Delayed or infrequent reporting
- Can't verify fund usage independently
- Trust the platform blindly

### What Lenders Don't Know

| Question | Traditional Answer |
|----------|-------------------|
| Where exactly are my funds? | Somewhere in the platform |
| How are specific loans performing? | Aggregate statistics only |
| Are repayments being processed correctly? | Trust us |
| What's happening in real-time? | Wait for monthly report |

### The Trust Problem

```
Lender → Deposits Funds → [BLACK BOX] → Maybe gets returns?

What happens inside?
• Loan allocation decisions: Opaque
• Collection processes: Opaque
• Reserve management: Opaque
• Fee calculations: Opaque
```

## Problem 3: Limited Access

### What Happens Today

Traditional platforms have barriers:
- Platform-dependent operations
- Office hours limitations
- Geographic restrictions
- Banking hour dependencies

### Access Constraints

| Barrier | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Time** | Operations during business hours |
| **Geography** | Platform available in limited regions |
| **Technical** | Dependent on platform uptime |
| **Financial** | High minimums exclude many |

### Modern Expectations vs. Reality

| Users Expect | Traditional P2P Offers |
|--------------|----------------------|
| 24/7 access | Business hours |
| Global availability | Regional limits |
| Instant transactions | Processing delays |
| Real-time updates | Periodic reports |

## Problem 4: Custodial Risk

### What Happens Today

Your funds are held by the platform:
- Not in your control
- Platform failure = your loss
- Hack affects everyone
- Regulatory seizure possible

### The Custody Problem

```
Your Money → Platform Account → Platform Controls
                    ↓
        [Platform bankruptcy]
                    ↓
         Your funds at risk
```

### Historical Examples

Platform failures have happened:
- Platforms shutting down with funds
- Hacks resulting in fund loss
- Fraud by platform operators
- Regulatory actions freezing accounts

## Problem 5: Poor Price Discovery

### What Happens Today

Without secondary markets:
- No way to value loans accurately
- Can't trade based on information
- Mispricing persists
- Capital allocation inefficient

### Market Inefficiencies

| Issue | Consequence |
|-------|-------------|
| No trading | Can't price risk dynamically |
| Stale valuations | Book value ≠ true value |
| Information asymmetry | Platform knows more than you |
| No competition | Accept platform terms or leave |

## Problem 6: Lack of Composability

### What Happens Today

Traditional P2P exists in isolation:
- Can't use investments as collateral
- No DeFi integration
- Limited product innovation
- Closed ecosystem

### Missed Opportunities

```
Traditional: Invest → Wait → Collect

Could be: Invest → Use as collateral → Earn additional yield
         → Trade positions → Compose with other protocols
```
