# Key Benefits

ReFlow transforms traditional P2P lending by solving its biggest pain points. Here's what makes us different.

## 1. Liquidity When You Need It

### The Old Way
- Funds locked for 6-12 months
- No exit until loan maturity
- Emergency? Too bad.

### The ReFlow Way
- Trade loan tokens on secondary market
- Exit positions anytime
- You control your liquidity

## 2. Complete Transparency

### The Old Way
- Limited visibility into fund usage
- Delayed reporting
- Trust the platform blindly

### The ReFlow Way
- Every transaction on-chain
- Real-time portfolio tracking
- Verify everything on blockchain explorer

## 3. 24/7 Access

### The Old Way
- Platform-dependent operations
- Office hours limitations
- Geographic restrictions

### The ReFlow Way
- Trade and manage anytime
- Access from anywhere
- Blockchain never sleeps

## 4. True Ownership

### The Old Way
- Platform holds your funds
- Custodial risk
- Platform failure = your loss

### The ReFlow Way
- Non-custodial wallets
- You hold your tokens
- Your keys, your assets

## 5. Flexible Investment Options

| Feature | Managed Mode | Self-Managed Mode |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Control** | Hands-off | Full control |
| **Time Required** | Minimal | Active management |
| **Diversification** | Automatic | Manual |
| **Best For** | Passive investors | Experienced investors |

## 6. Lower Barriers

- **Lower Minimums**: Fractional ownership of loans
- **Diversification**: Spread across multiple loans easily
- **Accessibility**: Just need a wallet and internet

- KYC/AML compliance
- Investor protections
