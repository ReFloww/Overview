# Partner Onboarding

Guide for new partners integrating with ReFlow.

## Partnership Types

### Technology Partner

Integrate ReFlow into your existing platform.

**Benefits:**
- API access
- Technical documentation
- Integration support
- Co-marketing opportunities

**Requirements:**
- Valid business entity
- Technical team capability
- Compliance with local regulations

### Lending Partner

Provide loans for tokenization on ReFlow.

**Benefits:**
- Access to blockchain liquidity
- Transparent loan management
- Secondary market for your loans
- Real-time reporting

**Requirements:**
- Licensed P2P lending platform
- Track record of loan performance
- Compliance documentation
- Due diligence completion

### Fund Manager Partner

Manage investment portfolios on ReFlow.

**Benefits:**
- Portfolio management tools
- Performance tracking
- Investor access
- Management fees

**Requirements:**
- Investment management experience
- Verifiable track record
- KYC/AML compliance
- Capital commitment

## Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Application

Submit partnership application:

1. Visit [partners.reflow.io](https://partners.reflow.io)
2. Select partnership type
3. Complete application form
4. Provide required documents

### Step 2: Due Diligence

Our team will review:

- Business registration
- Regulatory licenses
- Financial statements
- Track record
- Technical capabilities

Timeline: 1-2 weeks

### Step 3: Agreement

Upon approval:

1. Review partnership agreement
2. Negotiate terms if needed
3. Sign agreement
4. Receive API credentials

### Step 4: Technical Setup

Set up your integration:

1. Access developer documentation
2. Configure sandbox environment
3. Implement integration
4. Complete testing

### Step 5: Compliance Review

Final compliance check:

1. KYC/AML procedures
2. Data protection compliance
3. Disclosure requirements
4. Risk management

### Step 6: Go Live

Launch your integration:

1. Production credentials issued
2. Go-live checklist completed
3. Monitoring configured
4. Support channels established

## Required Documents

### All Partners

- Business registration certificate
- Company profile
- Proof of address
- Director/owner identification
- Bank account details

### Lending Partners (Additional)

- P2P lending license
- Loan portfolio summary
- Default rate history
- Audit reports (last 2 years)
- Risk assessment methodology

### Fund Managers (Additional)

- Investment management credentials
- Performance track record
- Investment strategy document
- Risk management policy
- Fee structure

## FAQ

**How long does onboarding take?**

Typically 4-8 weeks depending on partnership type and technical complexity.

**What are the costs?**

Partnership costs vary by type. Contact partnerships@reflow.io for details.

**Can I integrate without being a formal partner?**

Basic API access is available for approved developers. Full partnership provides additional benefits and support.

**What jurisdictions do you support?**

Currently focused on Indonesia and Southeast Asia. Contact us for other regions.
