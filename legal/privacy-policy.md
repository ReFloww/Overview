# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: January 2025*

This Privacy Policy describes how ReFlow ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Wallet addresses
- Email address (if provided)
- KYC information (through our verification partner)

**KYC Information (via third-party provider):**
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Government ID documents
- Proof of address
- Selfie/photo verification

**Communication Data:**
- Support requests
- Feedback and surveys
- Email correspondence

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Technical Data:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Operating system
- Referring URLs

**Usage Data:**
- Pages visited
- Features used
- Time spent on Platform
- Click patterns

**Blockchain Data:**
- Public wallet addresses
- Transaction history (publicly available on-chain)

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

- KYC verification results
- Compliance screening results
- Analytics data

## 2. How We Use Information

### 2.1 Provide Services

- Process transactions
- Display portfolio information
- Enable secondary market trading
- Send transaction notifications

### 2.2 Compliance

- Verify identity (KYC)
- Prevent fraud and money laundering
- Comply with legal requirements
- Respond to legal requests

### 2.3 Improve Platform

- Analyze usage patterns
- Fix bugs and issues
- Develop new features
- Optimize performance

### 2.4 Communications

- Send service notifications
- Respond to inquiries
- Provide customer support
- Send marketing (with consent)

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Duration of account + 5 years |
| KYC data | As required by law (typically 5-7 years) |
| Transaction records | 7 years |
| Support communications | 3 years |
| Analytics data | 2 years |

### 4.2 Deletion

When data is no longer needed, we:
- Delete or anonymize personal data
- Retain only what is legally required
- Maintain blockchain records (immutable)

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures

We implement:

- Encryption in transit (TLS)
- Encryption at rest
- Access controls
- Security monitoring
- Regular security audits
- Employee training

### 5.2 Incident Response

In case of data breach:

- We will investigate promptly
- Notify affected users as required
- Report to authorities as required
- Take remediation measures

### 5.3 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

- Securing your wallet and private keys
- Using strong passwords
- Keeping devices secure
- Reporting suspicious activity
