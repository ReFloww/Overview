# Cookie Policy

*Last Updated: January 2025*

This Cookie Policy explains how ReFlow uses cookies and similar technologies.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

**Purpose:** Required for the Platform to function properly.

**Examples:**
- Authentication state
- Session management
- Security tokens
- Wallet connection state

**Duration:** Session or up to 24 hours

**Can be disabled?** No - these are required for basic functionality.

### Analytics Cookies

**Purpose:** Help us understand how users interact with the Platform.

**Examples:**
- Page views
- Feature usage
- Navigation paths
- Error tracking

**Duration:** Up to 2 years

**Can be disabled?** Yes

### Preference Cookies

**Purpose:** Remember your settings and preferences.

**Examples:**
- Language preference
- Theme settings (dark/light mode)
- Display preferences
- Notification settings

**Duration:** Up to 1 year

**Can be disabled?** Yes, but your preferences won't be saved.

### Performance Cookies

**Purpose:** Help us improve Platform performance.

**Examples:**
- Load times
- Error rates
- API response times

**Duration:** Up to 1 year

**Can be disabled?** Yes

## Similar Technologies

### Local Storage

We use browser local storage to store:
- Wallet connection preferences
- UI state
- Cached data for performance

### Session Storage

Temporary storage for:
- Transaction state
- Form data
- Temporary preferences

## Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser:

**Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies

**Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies

**Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

**Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions

### Our Cookie Settings

Use our cookie consent tool to:
- View active cookies
- Accept or reject cookie categories
- Update preferences at any time

Access via the cookie icon in the footer.

### Do Not Track

We respect "Do Not Track" browser signals for analytics cookies.

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

| Cookie Type | Impact if Disabled |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Essential | Platform won't work properly |
| Analytics | We can't improve based on usage |
| Preferences | Settings won't be remembered |
| Performance | We can't optimize performance |

## Cookie Retention

| Cookie Type | Retention |
|-------------|-----------|
| Session cookies | Until browser closed |
| Persistent cookies | Up to 2 years |
| Local storage | Until cleared |
