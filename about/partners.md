## Lending Partner

### Restock.id

**Role:** Primary lending partner providing loan origination and borrower management.

**About:**
Restock.id is an OJK-licensed P2P lending platform operating in Indonesia. They specialize in productive loans for micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs).

**Partnership Scope:**
- Loan origination and underwriting
- Borrower verification and KYC
- Loan servicing and collection
- Regulatory compliance (OJK)

**Why Restock.id:**
- Licensed and regulated by OJK
- Proven track record in MSME lending
- Strong underwriting process
- Established borrower network
- Commitment to financial inclusion

[Learn more about the partnership](../compliance/restock-partnership.md)
