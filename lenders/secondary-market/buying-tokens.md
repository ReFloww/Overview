# Buying Loan Tokens

Buying tokens on the secondary market lets you access loans you might have missed during initial funding, or find undervalued opportunities.

## Why Buy on Secondary Market

### Advantages

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Missed Opportunities** | Access loans that funded quickly |
| **Shorter Duration** | Buy near-maturity for quicker returns |
| **Discounted Tokens** | Find deals from motivated sellers |
| **Portfolio Building** | Diversify more easily |
| **Yield Optimization** | Find better rates than current offerings |

## Evaluating Listings

### Key Information

Each listing displays:

| Field | What to Check |
|-------|---------------|
| **Price** | Is it fair value? |
| **Original Principal** | Base investment |
| **Accrued Interest** | Already earned interest |
| **APY** | Return rate |
| **Days Remaining** | Time to maturity |
| **Risk Grade** | Credit quality |
| **Loan Status** | Performance health |

### Calculating Value

```
Fair Value = Principal + Accrued Interest
Your Cost = Listed Price + Gas
Your Return = (Maturity Value - Your Cost) / Your Cost
```

### Example Analysis

```
Listing:
- Price: 950 USDT
- Principal Remaining: 900 USDT
- Accrued Interest: 60 USDT
- APY: 12%
- Days Remaining: 30

Analysis:
- Fair Value: 900 + 60 = 960 USDT
- Discount: 10 USDT (1%)
- Value at Maturity: ~969 USDT
- Your Return: (969 - 950) / 950 = 2% in 30 days
```

## Buying Process

### Step 1: Find a Listing

1. Browse secondary market
2. Apply desired filters
3. Review multiple options
4. Compare prices and terms

### Step 2: Review Details

Click on listing to see:
- Full loan information
- Borrower sector
- Payment history
- Risk assessment
- Seller's price justification

### Step 3: Decide to Buy

Check:
- [ ] Price is fair or attractive
- [ ] Loan is performing well
- [ ] Duration fits your needs
- [ ] Risk grade is acceptable

### Step 4: Execute Purchase

1. Click **"Buy"**
2. Review transaction summary
3. Approve USDT spending (if first time)
4. Confirm purchase
5. Sign transaction in wallet

### Step 5: Confirmation

After purchase:
- Tokens appear in your wallet
- Listed in your portfolio
- You're now the owner of that loan share

## After Purchase

### What You Own

- Proportional share of loan
- Right to remaining repayments
- Can sell again on secondary market

### Tracking Your Purchase

1. Go to **Portfolio**
2. Find new position
3. Track performance
4. Receive repayments

### Repayments

You'll receive:
- Your share of each repayment
- Proportional interest
- Full principal at maturity

## Strategies for Buying

### Value Buying

Look for:
- Motivated sellers offering discounts
- Performing loans below fair value
- Temporary market inefficiencies

### Near-Maturity Strategy

Benefits:
- Lower duration risk
- Quick return of capital
- Known outcome timeline

Considerations:
- Lower absolute returns
- Less time to compound

### Yield Enhancement

If current offerings have low rates:
- Search secondary for older, higher-rate loans
- Lock in better returns
- Diversify yield sources

### Diversification

Use secondary market to:
- Add different sectors
- Balance risk grades
- Vary durations

## Risks to Consider

### Purchase Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Overpaying** | Calculate fair value first |
| **Hidden Problems** | Review loan performance |
| **Default** | Check payment history |
| **Illiquidity** | May be hard to resell |

### Due Diligence Checklist

Before buying, verify:
- [ ] Loan is current on payments
- [ ] No default warnings
- [ ] Sector outlook is stable
- [ ] Price makes sense
- [ ] Fits portfolio strategy

## Common Mistakes

### 1. Ignoring Loan Status

Don't buy based on price alone. Always check if the loan is performing.

### 2. Not Calculating Returns

Know your actual return after purchase price and fees.

### 3. Concentration

Don't put too much in one secondary purchase.

### 4. Chasing High APY

Higher rates often mean higher risk. Understand why.

## Tips for Success

1. **Be Patient**: Good deals come to those who wait
2. **Set Alerts**: Get notified for specific listings
3. **Know Your Limits**: Set maximum prices before shopping
4. **Start Small**: Test with smaller purchases first
5. **Track Everything**: Record purchase prices and reasoning
