## Indonesian Regulatory Framework

### OJK (Otoritas Jasa Keuangan)

Indonesia's Financial Services Authority regulates:

| Aspect | Regulation |
|--------|------------|
| **P2P Lending** | POJK 77/POJK.01/2016 |
| **Consumer Protection** | POJK 6/POJK.07/2022 |
| **Data Privacy** | Multiple regulations |
| **AML/CFT** | POJK requirements |

### Key Requirements

#### Licensing
- Registered P2P lending platform
- Regular reporting to OJK
- Compliance audits
- Operating standards

#### Investor Protection
- Information disclosure
- Risk warnings
- Complaint handling
- Fund segregation

#### Operational Standards
- Minimum capital requirements
- Technology standards
- Business continuity
- Record keeping

## KYC/AML Framework

### Know Your Customer (KYC)

| Level | Requirements |
|-------|--------------|
| **Basic** | Email, phone verification |
| **Standard** | ID document, selfie, address |
| **Enhanced** | Additional verification for large amounts |

### Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

| Measure | Implementation |
|---------|---------------|
| **Customer Due Diligence** | KYC process |
| **Transaction Monitoring** | Automated systems |
| **Suspicious Activity** | Reporting procedures |
| **Record Keeping** | As per regulations |

See [KYC/AML Compliance](kyc-aml.md) for details.

## Blockchain Regulatory Considerations

### Current Status

Blockchain and crypto regulations vary by jurisdiction. ReFlow:
- Operates on public blockchain (transparency)
- Uses regulated partner (Restock.id)
- Implements compliance measures
- Monitors regulatory developments

### Self-Custody Model

| Aspect | Regulatory Implication |
|--------|----------------------|
| **Non-Custodial** | ReFlow doesn't hold user funds |
| **User Control** | Users responsible for wallet security |
| **Transparency** | All transactions verifiable |
| **Smart Contracts** | Automated, rules-based operations |

## Data Protection

### Personal Data Handling
