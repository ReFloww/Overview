# Risk Disclosures

This document provides comprehensive risk disclosures for ReFlow users. Understanding these risks is essential before investing.

## Important Notice

**Investment in P2P loans involves significant risk, including the potential loss of your entire investment. Only invest funds you can afford to lose. Past performance does not guarantee future results.**

## Investment Risks

### Credit Risk (Default Risk)

**Description**: Borrowers may fail to repay their loans partially or in full.

| Severity | High |
|----------|------|
| **Impact** | Loss of principal and interest |
| **Likelihood** | Varies by loan grade |
| **Mitigation** | Diversification, grade selection |

**What Can Happen**:
- Partial principal loss
- Total principal loss
- Lost interest payments
- Extended recovery process

### Liquidity Risk

**Description**: You may not be able to sell your tokens when you want to.

| Severity | Medium-High |
|----------|-------------|
| **Impact** | Inability to exit position |
| **Likelihood** | Varies with market conditions |
| **Mitigation** | Don't invest emergency funds |

**What Can Happen**:
- No buyers available
- Need to sell at significant discount
- Funds locked until maturity

### Interest Rate Risk

**Description**: Market rates may change after you invest.

| Severity | Low-Medium |
|----------|------------|
| **Impact** | Opportunity cost |
| **Likelihood** | Moderate |
| **Mitigation** | Duration laddering |

**What Can Happen**:
- Locked into below-market rates
- Secondary market value affected

### Concentration Risk

**Description**: Too much invested in a single loan, sector, or grade.

| Severity | Medium-High |
|----------|-------------|
| **Impact** | Amplified losses |
| **Likelihood** | User-dependent |
| **Mitigation** | Diversification discipline |

**What Can Happen**:
- Single default significantly impacts portfolio
- Sector downturn affects multiple positions

## Risk Mitigation Guidelines

### Do

- Diversify across multiple loans
- Start with small amounts
- Understand what you're investing in
- Keep most funds liquid
- Secure your wallet properly
- Stay informed about platform updates

### Don't

- Invest more than you can lose
- Put emergency funds in P2P loans
- Concentrate in single loans/sectors
- Ignore risk grades
- Share wallet credentials
- Assume guaranteed returns
