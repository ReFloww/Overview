## Declaration Statement

This document serves as the official compliance declaration for ReFlow, a decentralized P2P lending platform for Real World Assets (RWA) built on Mantle Network.

### Does this project involve regulated assets?

**Yes.** ReFlow involves regulated financial assets.

---

## Nature of Regulated Assets

### 1. Tokenized P2P Loans

ReFlow tokenizes peer-to-peer loans into ERC-20 tokens. These tokens represent:
- Fractional ownership of underlying loan receivables
- Rights to principal and interest repayments
- Tradeable securities on secondary markets

### 2. Real World Assets (RWA)

The underlying loans are backed by real-world assets in sectors including:
- Agriculture
- Fisheries
- Forestry
- SME working capital

## Risk Disclosures

### Investment Risks

1. **Credit Risk**: Borrowers may default on loan obligations
2. **Liquidity Risk**: Secondary market liquidity not guaranteed
3. **Smart Contract Risk**: Potential vulnerabilities in blockchain code
4. **Regulatory Risk**: Changes in regulations may affect operations
5. **Market Risk**: Token values may fluctuate

### Platform Risks

1. **Technology Risk**: System failures or cyber attacks
2. **Operational Risk**: Partner (Restock.id) operational issues
3. **Blockchain Risk**: Network congestion, forks, or failures
