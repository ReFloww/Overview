# KYC Verification

Know Your Customer (KYC) verification is required to invest on ReFlow. This process ensures regulatory compliance and protects all platform users.

## Why KYC Is Required

### Regulatory Compliance
- Required by OJK (Indonesian Financial Services Authority)
- Anti-money laundering (AML) regulations
- Investor protection laws

### Platform Security
- Prevents fraudulent accounts
- Enables dispute resolution
- Builds trust in the ecosystem

## Verification Levels

| Level | Requirements | Access |
|-------|--------------|--------|
| **Basic** | Email, Phone | View platform |
| **Standard** | ID Document, Selfie | Invest up to limit |
| **Enhanced** | Additional documents | Unlimited investing |

## Required Documents

### Standard Verification

You'll need one of the following:

**Primary ID (choose one)**
- National ID Card (KTP for Indonesian users)
- Passport
- Driver's License

**Supporting Information**
- Current selfie (for liveness check)
- Email address
- Phone number

### Enhanced Verification (if requested)

Additional documents may include:
- Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
- Source of funds declaration
- Additional ID documents

## Step-by-Step Verification

### Step 1: Access Verification

1. Log in to ReFlow
2. Navigate to **Profile** → **Verification**
3. Click **"Start Verification"**

### Step 2: Personal Information

Fill in your details:

```
Personal Details
├── Full Legal Name (as on ID)
├── Date of Birth
├── Nationality
├── Address
│   ├── Street Address
│   ├── City
│   ├── Province/State
│   └── Postal Code
└── Tax ID (if applicable)
```

### Step 3: Document Upload

**ID Document**
1. Select document type
2. Take clear photo of front
3. Take clear photo of back (if applicable)
4. Ensure all text is readable

**Photo Requirements**
- Clear, well-lit image
- All corners visible
- No glare or shadows
- Full document in frame

### Step 4: Selfie Verification

1. Position face in the frame
2. Ensure good lighting
3. Remove glasses/hats
4. Follow on-screen instructions
5. May include liveness check (turn head, blink)

### Step 5: Submit and Wait

1. Review all information
2. Confirm accuracy
3. Submit for review
4. Wait for approval notification

## Verification Timeline

| Status | Expected Duration |
|--------|-------------------|
| Submitted | Immediate |
| Under Review | 24-48 hours |
| Approved | Instant notification |
| Rejected | With reason and next steps |

## Common Rejection Reasons

### Document Issues
- Blurry or unclear images
- Expired documents
- Incomplete document capture
- Damaged or obscured documents

### Information Mismatch
- Name doesn't match ID
- Date of birth incorrect
- Address verification failed

### Selfie Issues
- Face not clearly visible
- Liveness check failed
- Photos don't match ID

## Resubmission Process

If rejected:

1. Read rejection reason carefully
2. Gather correct documents
3. Go to **Profile** → **Verification**
4. Click **"Resubmit"**
5. Follow corrected process

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does verification take?**
Usually 24-48 hours. Complex cases may take longer.

**Can I invest while waiting for KYC?**
No. Full KYC approval is required before investing.

**What if my name changed?**
Contact support with legal documentation of name change.

**Is my data shared with third parties?**
Only with verification partners and as required by law.
