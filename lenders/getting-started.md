# Getting Started as a Lender

Welcome to ReFlow! This guide walks you through everything you need to start lending and earning returns on your investments.

## Overview

Becoming a lender on ReFlow involves three main steps:

1. **Create Your Account** - Connect your wallet
2. **Complete KYC** - Verify your identity
3. **Start Investing** - Choose loans or fund managers

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

### Required
- [ ] A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet, etc.)
- [ ] MNT tokens for transaction fees
- [ ] USDT for investments
- [ ] Valid government-issued ID

### Recommended
- [ ] Basic understanding of P2P lending
- [ ] Investment goals in mind
- [ ] Risk tolerance assessed

## The Onboarding Process

```
Connect Wallet → Create Profile → KYC Verification → Fund Account → Start Lending
```

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit the ReFlow platform
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top right
3. Select your wallet provider
4. Approve the connection in your wallet
5. Sign the authentication message

### Step 2: Create Your Profile

After connecting, complete your profile:

- Display name (optional)
- Email address (for notifications)
- Communication preferences

### Step 3: Complete KYC Verification

KYC (Know Your Customer) is required for compliance:

1. Go to **Profile** → **Verification**
2. Fill in personal details
3. Upload ID document
4. Take a selfie for verification
5. Submit and wait for approval

*Verification typically takes 24-48 hours.*

### Step 4: Fund Your Account

1. Ensure your wallet has:
   - **USDT** for investments
   - **MNT** for gas fees
2. Add Mantle Network to your wallet if needed
3. Transfer tokens to your connected wallet

### Step 5: Start Lending

Choose your investment approach:

**Option A: Managed Mode**
- Navigate to Auto-Manage
- Select a fund manager
- Invest your desired amount

**Option B: Self-Managed Mode**
- Browse the Market
- Select loan products
- Invest in individual loans

## Account Security

### Best Practices

| Do | Don't |
|-----|-------|
| Use a hardware wallet for large amounts | Share your private keys |
| Enable all security features | Use public WiFi for transactions |
| Verify all transaction details | Rush through confirmations |
| Keep recovery phrase offline | Store credentials digitally |

### Two-Factor Authentication

We recommend enabling 2FA for additional security:

1. Go to **Profile** → **Security**
2. Enable two-factor authentication
3. Save backup codes securely

## Understanding Your Dashboard

Once set up, your dashboard shows:

- **Total Portfolio Value**: Your total investment value
- **Active Investments**: Number of active positions
- **Returns**: Earned and projected returns
- **Recent Activity**: Latest transactions
